# Map

> [!summary]
> The geographical guide to Elezar and the places beyond its physical world.

## Physical World

- [[Continents]] — the largest land divisions.
- [[Regions]] — territories and broad areas within continents.
- [[Settlements]] — cities, towns, villages, fortresses, and inhabited sites.
- [[Landmarks]] — unique or culturally significant places.
- [[Natural Features]] — forests, mountains, seas, deserts, rivers, and other natural formations.

## Other Realms

- [[Realms and Planes]] — non-ordinary spaces such as the Divine Realm, Heaven, and Hell.

## Navigation Principle

A place note should describe where something is and what exists there. Its complete historical narrative belongs in [[History]], while cultural meaning belongs in [[Cultures]] or [[Stories and Legends]].
