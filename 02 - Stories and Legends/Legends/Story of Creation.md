# Story of Creation

> [!summary]
> The ancient tale of how our world came to be.

## Cultural Context

This Fable is the most important story in our world. No one—not even the [[Thrones|living Thrones]]—knows how we first came to know it.

It is said that the [[Primordial|Primordial One]] themself bestowed it upon us. Because of this, every sentient being feels an innate familiarity with the Fable. Although its exact wording differs between languages and cultures, the story itself always remains the same.

Whenever someone attempts to alter it, anyone who hears the false version—even someone who has never heard the Fable before—instinctively knows that it is not true.

## Narrative

### The Arrival of the Primordial

In the time before time, the world was a lifeless stone suspended in an endless void. Then came the [[Primordial]], the First God, whose power knew no bounds. With a single gesture, they opened the floodgates of creation, submerging the barren rock beneath a vast ocean.

### The Rise of Alfeos and Omeos

From the depths, two lands emerged: [[Alfeos]], a realm rich with the potential for harmony, and [[Omeos]], a harsh domain of chaos and survival. Alfeos promised balance, while Omeos embraced the struggle for existence, setting the stage for the generations to come.

### The Tears of Life

Yet the world remained silent.

Moved by loneliness, the Primordial wept two tears, each brimming with divine essence. As they fell, they infused the lands with [[Magic|Mana]], awakening life in both Alfeos and Omeos.

### The Awakening of Creatures

In Alfeos, vibrant plants and simple animals began to flourish under the nurturing touch of Mana.

Meanwhile, Omeos gave birth to fierce beasts and monstrous entities, each fighting for dominance in a brutal world.

### The World Awakens

As life blossomed across both continents, Mana flowed through the earth, linking all living things. The stage was set for the rise of sentient beings, and the balance between creation and chaos began to take shape.
