# Stories and Legends

> [!summary]
> The tales through which the people of Elezar understand their world, remember history, express belief, and preserve cultural identity.

## Foundational Story

- [[Story of Creation]] — the universally familiar tale of how the world came to be.

## Collections

- [[Fables]] — major narrative accounts of Cataclysms, Miracles, ascensions, and other world-shaping acts.
- [[Folklore]] — local legends, superstitions, oral traditions, monsters, and disputed tales.
- [[Songs]] — hymns, ballads, poems, chants, and musical histories.
- [[Art]] — paintings, statues, symbols, theatre, tapestries, and other visual or performed traditions.

## Distinction from History

A note in [[History]] records what is presently accepted to have happened.

A note here records **how people tell, remember, interpret, or transform what happened**.

For example:

- Historical record: `[[First Cataclysm]]`
- Traditional retelling: `[[Fable of the First Cataclysm]]`
