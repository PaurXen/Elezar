## Classification Rules

- **Era:** a vast historical division defined by a change in the state of the world.
- **Period:** a smaller phase within an era.
- **War:** a prolonged organized conflict consisting of many events.
- **Event:** a discrete occurrence with causes, participants, and consequences.
- **Story:** a cultural retelling and therefore belongs in [[Stories and Legends]].
