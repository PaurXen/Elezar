## What a Race Note Should Describe

- origin,
- physical nature,
- lifespan,
- inherent abilities,
- variants,
- geographical distribution,
- relationship with magic,
- related cultures.

## Important Distinction

A race does not automatically possess one culture.

For example:

```text
[[Elves]]
├── may belong to → [[Doriathu Culture]]
├── may live outside → [[Doriathu]]
└── may develop other cultures
```
