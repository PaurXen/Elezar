# Monarchs

> [!summary]
> The dominant rulers who rose from the violent order of Omeos.

## The Four Monarchs

- [[Horde]] — hobgoblin ruler of the North.
- [[Rotten Ruler]] — lich ruler of the East.
- [[Demon Lord]] — demon ruler of the South.
- [[Hellish Heat]] — fire giant ruler of the West.

## Unofficial Fifth Monarch

- [[Night Blade]] — protector of [[Arcadia]], who rejected the violent rule of the other Monarchs.
