## Character Organization

A character has only one canonical note, even when they belong to several factions.

Example:

```text
[[Arthur Asedolon]]
├── character
├── ruler of → [[Empire of Asedolon]]
├── connected to → [[Great Magic Academy]]
└── ascended as → [[King of Kings]]
```

Other notes should link to that character rather than creating copies.
