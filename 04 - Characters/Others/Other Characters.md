# Other Characters

> [!summary]
> Characters who are not primarily organized as Throne-holders or Monarchs.

## By Faction or Affiliation

Create subfolders here only when a group has enough characters to justify one.

Possible examples:

- `Asedolon/`
- `Doriathu/`
- `Karaz-a-Karak/`
- `Great Magic Academy/`
- `Arcadia/`

## Rule

A character note belongs in only one physical location. Other affiliations should be represented through links and properties.
