# Factions

> [!summary]
> The political, religious, organizational, and familial powers that act within Elezar.

## Categories

- [[Organizations]] — institutions, academies, military orders, councils, and formal groups.
- [[Cults]] — religious movements and groups centered on worship, doctrine, or divine figures.
- [[Nations]] — territorial states, kingdoms, empires, and independent political communities.
- [[Dynasties]] — ruling or historically significant bloodlines.

## Important Distinctions

- A **faction** is an organized power or collective actor.
- A **culture** is a shared way of life.
- A **race** is a biological or metaphysical people.
- A **nation** is a political and territorial entity.
- A **dynasty** is a family line.

These may overlap but should not be treated as the same thing.
