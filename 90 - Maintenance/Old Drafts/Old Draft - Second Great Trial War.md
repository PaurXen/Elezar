# Old Draft - Second Great Trial War

> [!warning]
> Superseded material preserved from the original manuscript.

3rd Cataclysm created an unimaginable army of undead that advanced into the continent as the ground rotted under their feet. Armies of Asedolon were pushed back by the huge number of the undead and Hoard’s armies.

As undead marched forward corruption started to spread. King of Kings asked for help from both mages of Great Magic Academy as well as dwarves of Karaz-a-Karak to reinforce the front line.

Slowly but surely corruption began to spread across the continent and to prevent it the elven sage, Priest of the Nature took it upon himself to cure the continent of this rot. He sat in the heart of the Enchanted Forest, a vain of the world, and became one with life and nature becoming a sapling. A sapling of the World Tree, which became the 9th throne changed the Mark of Nineteen from eleven to ten, and later the World Tree was named the 5th miracle. World Tree started stopping the curse that Monarch of the Dead cast preventing further birth of the new undead but the curing process was slow and forward the new throne living cataclysm was headed.

While World Tree slowly was curing the curse it was still weak and unprotected from destruction that was approaching. The 4th Monarch, Hellish Heat started approaching the heart of the forest thus he started approaching a new miracle, the World Tree, it was then that a close friend of Priest of the Nature named himself Guardian of the Tree and went to confront Hellish Heat and his army. During the 4th Monarch’s march entire eastern part of the forest was burned that’s why Guardian of the Tree chose it as his battlefield. During this fight army of Hellish Heat was nearly obliterated but the elven protector couldn’t defeat the monarch so he reached for the power of the world and the World Tree. The ground shook, and the land sunk and parted, repelling everything towards the east as a hurricane created by the gods themselves. Guardian of the Tree chose to sacrifice the south-eastern part of the continent as it was devoured by flames of the destruction of Hellish Heat thus he cut off it from the continent, creating a small island and blowing 4th monarch a hundred kilometres to the east, behind the new ocean void. This incident was marked as the 5th cataclysm, causing the ascension of Guardian of the Tree to the 10th throne, as Guardian of the Mundus changing the Mark of Nineteen from ten to nine, engraving the next cataclysm into everyone's mind. Guardian of the Tree then returned to the World Tree and remains there to this day.

After the 5th cataclysm, the King of Kings gathered every leader of Alfeos during the Final Conference to discuss matters of the war. The conference lasted nineteen days and ended with one conclusion; We can’t win so we won’t lose. Alfeos gathered everyone able to fight and sent them to the front lines to stop the advance and determine where the Line would be. When the position was determined line was drowned on the map of Alfeos and given to a dwarven archmage from Great Magic Academe, Conjurer of Mountains. He flew to the center of the frontline and started chanding. Reaching his left head forward beginning of the final mountains and his right head forward edge of the new ocean void and then he closed his hands in and like lifting something heavy he lifted the ground itself. Creating a mountain range that divided the eastern and western parts of the continent leaving only a small gap in the middle. This godly act, the 6th miracle was enough to earn him the 11th throne, giving him the name Terra Divisor.

Thus 2nd Great Trial War ended dividing the continent by Sacred mountains into the left side that Alfeos ruled and the right side where Omeos rotted.
