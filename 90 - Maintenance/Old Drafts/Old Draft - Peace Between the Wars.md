# Old Draft - Peace Between the Wars

> [!warning]
> Superseded material preserved from the original manuscript.

After over 50 years of 1st GTW Alfeos could again taste peace. The war ended and countries started to stabilize, at least they tried. In Karaz-a-Karak there was an uprising. The dwarven king ordered Dwarfcules to attack dragons' territory and that caused the dwarven kingdom to lose their territory The hero was executed by his son who manipulated the people of his country into supporting him. He sized the dwarven throne and started his iron-handed rule. In the next few years, he was deemed a tyrant and nobles started planning new uprisings that unfortunately never came to be. Nobles that conspired against him were captured and executed together with their whole bloodlines just like every participant of the forming rebellion. That day streets and water canals were painted red and the smell of iron filled the air marking that day as Red Iron Fall. The whole world became aware of this incident as its image was burned into everyone’s mind together with the title Ruby Heart and thus just like the dwarven throne, the 4th throne was sized changing mark from sixteen to fifteen and also earning tyrant king another title, Bloody Tyrant. He was the first divine throne lived and stayed after ascension only after his death from old age he was brought to the Primordial.

When dwarves were fighting themselves a scholar of elves Great Sage left the elves and departed together with his disciples to the newly created by 1st Cataclysm island. He knew Great Mage and refused to fight humans so after the war he left. Together with his disciples, he explored and started researching the power of the divine. They settled on this island and with time the words of his research spread as the new mages and scholars started to arrive. When more people started to seek him and learn he realized that knowledge should be shared and not restricted to species like elves did hiding their knowledge from even themselves. So he opened a school in which everybody could learn of magic and the divine. 40 years after 1st GTW his school became the Great Academy of Magic. And while Great Sage was giving a lecture the Mark changed from fifteen to fourteen and everyone learned about the Great Sage's creation together with the title Sage of Futurum, marking the next ascension. Thus Great Sage, Sage of Futurum sized 5th throne.

While the people of Alfeos tried to restore, the people of Omeos tried to survive. After Primal Call already deadly and chaotic continent became a living hell. The Four Monarchs started invading every territory that they could and slathering everyone that they could together with their armies. No place was safe, except one. At the heart of the continent in the land that no monarch wanted a lone dark knight demon created an oasis for those tired of battle, an Arcadia for demons. Night Blade was a protector of Arcadia, whoever tried to cause harm to his people would be disposed of. His strength was on par with monarchs so during 60 years of chaos that Primal Call caused he was able to defend his land but the day came when one of the Four Monarchs appeared, the 3rd Monarch, Demon Lord. Together with his army 3rd Monarch invaded Arcadia and the ones who opposed them were the people of Arcadia and Night Blade. The fight was fierce and Arcadia was losing. Night Blade ordered civilians and his forces to retreat and run while he held the 3rd Monarch and his army off. Night Blade fought the whole army nearly wiping them out until Demon Lord stepped in. They fought for days and Night Blade came victorious but gravely injured. It was the moment when the Mark turned from fourteen to thirteen and Night Blade sized the 6th throne as Sword of Arcadia. That was the day  when Alfeos learned of existence of the other continent.

During days of stabilization a young prince was born and his name was Arthur Asedolon, he was 3rd in the line for throne but he was extremely bright. When he was 12 he witnace ascension of Great Mage and he understood that like that school his country, no his continent needed unity. He started building his influence among nobility, citizens, elves and even Bloody Tyrant, one of the thrones on Elezar gave him his approval. He was prodigy and everybody saw him as the next ruler. When he was 20 another ascension took place but not in the unknown continent, a great warrior Sword of the Arcadia ascended but that bore questions of the unknown. Year after he sized the throne of the Empire and during his reign he united continent. Introduced elves to the outside of their forest, sign treaty with Ruby Heart and made him more merciful, brought order to No Man’s Land and formally introduce Great Magic Academy as school for everyone both rich and poor, humans, elves, dwarves, and other races that lived on Alfeos that sought knowledge. He started to be called Golden Ruler and  when he turned 30 on ball celebrating it the Mark of Nineteen change from thirteen  to twelve and divine name King of Kings as well as godly act in form of peace and unity that he saw, cared and reaped engraved in minds of all.
