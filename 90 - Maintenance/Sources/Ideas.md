[[Mana Fumes|Magic Residuum]]
### Great battles

Large-scale spellcasting could create temporary **ozone storms**:

- bluish haze;
- metallic-smelling air;
- respiratory danger;
- damaged crops;
- bleached leaves;
- unusually intense electrical activity.

### Divine or Throne-level magic

A cataclysm could inject enough ozone and magical oxygen into the atmosphere to alter local weather temporarily.
