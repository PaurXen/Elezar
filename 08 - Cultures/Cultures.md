# Cultures

> [!summary]
> The traditions, customs, beliefs, languages, identities, and ways of life practiced by the peoples of Elezar.

## Purpose

Cultures are usually reached through another subject:

- a race,
- a nation,
- a region,
- a religion,
- a faction,
- a historical period.

This folder remains a main category because culture connects all of those subjects without being identical to any of them.

## What a Culture Note Should Describe

- values and social expectations,
- language and naming,
- religion and philosophy,
- family and social structure,
- food and clothing,
- art and music,
- rites and festivals,
- treatment of magic,
- historical identity,
- regional and class differences.

## Important Distinctions

```text
Race ≠ Culture
Nation ≠ Culture
Faction ≠ Culture
Religion ≠ Culture
```

A culture may be influenced by all of them.
