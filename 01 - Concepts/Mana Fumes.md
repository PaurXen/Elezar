# Mana Fumes

> [!summary]
> Residuum left by energized [[Magic|Mana]] when it comes in contact with air [ozone].

To be expanded.

## Rules and Properties

[[Mana Fumes]] are transparent but can be recognized by the clean, crisp smell similar to that during a storm. They are mostly harmless but long exposure in fume rich environment or in enclosed space can cause:
- coughing
- burning eyes
- headaches
- damage to the lungs after long exposure

## Historical Development

[[Mana Fumes]] can be commonly encountered inside magic workshops and alchemical labs, as well as during long magical rituals. Fumes themself posses cleansing properties and because of that they are used at infirmaries or during [[house cleansing]] rituals. For production of fumes for emporiums are mostly responsible local [[Magic Academies]] or magical workshops.
