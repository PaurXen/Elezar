# Seasons

## Definition

To be expanded.

## Rules and Properties

### Spring

- sky: bright blue-cyan
- auroras: soft green and silver at night
- feeling: fresh, lucid, awakening

### Summer

- sky: rich cerulean with turquoise height
- sunsets: long gold-magenta-violet transitions
- auroras: weaker but still visible in high regions
- feeling: radiant, dream-clear, almost crystalline

### Autumn

- sky: deeper blue with more violet undertones
- sunsets: dramatic crimson, copper, lavender
- auroras: begin strengthening again
- feeling: wistful, reflective, mythic

### Winter

- sky: pale silver-blue by day
- upper sky: indigo-violet
- nights: strongest auroras
- feeling: silent, sacred, almost unreal
