# Afterlife

> [!summary]
> The complete metaphysical system through which souls pass after death.

## Overview

The Afterlife is the central concept connecting [[Souls]], [[Heaven]], [[Hell]], and [[Redemption]]. This note should explain the system as a whole, while the linked notes describe its individual parts.

## Structure

- [[Souls]] — what passes into the Afterlife.
- [[Heaven]] — the destination associated with peace and final rest.
- [[Hell]] — the realm associated with punishment, purification, or unresolved burdens.
- [[Redemption]] — the path through which a soul may be cleansed or renewed.

## Divine Custodians

- [[Custos Caeli]] — Sustainer of the Afterlife.
- [[Custos Inferni]] — Sustainer of Redemption.
