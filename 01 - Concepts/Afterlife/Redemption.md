# Redemption

> [!summary]
> The process through which burdened or condemned souls may be purified and made capable of peace.

## Purpose

Redemption should describe a **process**, not merely a place. It connects [[Hell]] with the possibility of reaching [[Heaven]].

## Questions to Define

- Who decides whether a soul can be redeemed?
- Must the soul willingly seek redemption?
- Is redemption punishment, understanding, transformation, or all three?
- Can some actions make redemption impossible?
- What authority does [[Custos Inferni]] possess over the process?
