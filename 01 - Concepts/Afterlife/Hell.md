# Hell

> [!summary]
> The part of the Afterlife associated with burden, punishment, purification, and the possibility of redemption.

## Overview

Hell should not automatically be treated as simple eternal damnation. Its exact purpose should be defined through its relationship with [[Redemption]] and [[Custos Inferni]].
