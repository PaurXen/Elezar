# Souls

> [!summary]
> The spiritual essence of sentient beings and the part of them that may continue after death.

## Nature of a Soul

To be defined:

- origin,
- connection to the body,
- connection to [[Magic]],
- memory and identity,
- death and separation,
- corruption or purification.

## Journey After Death

[[Souls]] → [[Afterlife]] → [[Heaven]] or [[Hell]] → possible [[Redemption]]
