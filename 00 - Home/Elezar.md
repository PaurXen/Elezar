## Vault Principles

1. Every subject has one main note.
2. Folders determine where a note lives; links show everything it belongs to.
3. Historical events and their legendary retellings remain separate.
4. Race, culture, nation, and faction are related but not interchangeable.
5. Uncertain information should be marked rather than silently treated as canon.
